ABOUT ME

Professionally

I am a Software Engineer with a focus in Front End Web Development. At my previous job, I primarily worked on the consumer websites 4moms.com and global.4moms.com, as well as the eCommerce website shop.4moms.com, totalling 19 websites.

During my time at 4moms, I played a significant role rebuilding all 19 sites to be responsive. This increased mobile traffic by 45% and mobile conversion rate by 25% within 6 weeks of completion of implementation. I also created a web application that automated documentation of product information coming off of the assembly line. This site functioned offline using application cache and local storage. 4moms went from capturing 0 to 15,000 product assemblies per month without a cost increase.
